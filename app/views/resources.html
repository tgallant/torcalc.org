Bandwidth Allocation Resources

There seems to be a split mind in regards to bandwidth allocation for tor relays. Some say it is best to ration your bandwidth throughout the month and others say it is best to use up your bandwidth as fast as you can and hibernate until your data usage is reset.

The following are some links collected from the tor mailing list and the tor wiki that are in favor of hibernation.

  • https://lists.torproject.org/pipermail/tor-dev/2014-September/007559.html
  • https://lists.torproject.org/pipermail/tor-dev/2014-September/007560.html
  • https://lists.torproject.org/pipermail/tor-relays/2014-November/005850.html
  • https://lists.torproject.org/pipermail/tor-relays/2011-September/000951.html
  • https://www.torproject.org/docs/faq.html.en#LimitTotalBandwidth
  • https://www.torproject.org/docs/faq.html.en#BandwidthShaping
